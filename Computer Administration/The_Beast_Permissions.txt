The_Beast_Permissions
	login and access info for 'the beast'

To find/set permissions: Login as Admin (username/passwords in envelope ask tanja). Right click on a folder and choose 'sharing and security'> security tab.

Lab Members (no password) have access to C:\Documents and Settings\Lab Member\Desktop and  C:\Documents and Settings\Lab Member\[usernames] documents (which looks like "my documents" to them).
